The Artpop Era

The artpop era has been, by far, one of the weirdest Lady Gaga eras. As described by Gaga her self, the artpop era is, "a celebration and a poetic musical journey." This era is heavily influenced by Greek and Roman mythology, combining aspects of both classical jazz and electronic music!! Some of my favorite songs from this era include "Applause" "Jewls n' drugs" "Do what u want" and "Gypsy." What makes this era extra special for me, is the fact that i got the chance to go PARTYYYYY at the ArtRAVE!!!!
